Michael Barringer

Director & Creative Producer
michael@michaelbarringer.net

Michael Barringer is a director and creative producer specializing in ambitious, large-scale storytelling.

He currently serves as Senior Manager, Creative Content Production at Sphere Entertainment, where he co-leads the in-house production team behind campaigns and original content for the Las Vegas Sphere.

His work spans immersive film launches, global marketing campaigns, and behind-the-scenes documentaries—including Darren Aronofsky’s Postcard from Earth, The Wizard of Oz at Sphere, and the upcoming Sphere Studios film From the Edge, directed by Jimmy Chin and Elizabeth Chai Vasarhelyi.

Michael is drawn to projects that sit at the intersection of story and spectacle. He works comfortably at 30,000 feet—defining themes, tone, and purpose—and on the ground, directing interviews, shaping performances, and building the edit from the inside.

He wrote and produced the award-winning short I Approve This Message, winner of the Audience Award at Dances With Films and earned official consideration at the 2025 Academy Awards.

He holds an MFA in Directing from the Feirstein Graduate School of Cinema.
